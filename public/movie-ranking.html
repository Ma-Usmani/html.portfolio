The Best Movies According to Me

My top 3 movies of all-time.

Green Book

A heartwarming tale of an unlikely friendship between a black pianist and his white chauffeur as they embark on a concert tour through the racially segregated South in the 1960s, Green Book is a beautifully crafted film that celebrates the triumph of humanity over prejudice.

All is Well

A poignant Indian drama-comedy that explores family dynamics, relationships, and the complexities of life through a journey of self-discovery, forgiveness, and redemption. With its blend of humor and emotional depth, All is Well resonates with audiences on a universal level.

The Wolf of Wall Street

Directed by Martin Scorsese, this electrifying biographical black comedy is a wild ride through the excesses and decadence of Wall Street in the 1980s. With Leonardo DiCaprio delivering a powerhouse performance, The Wolf of Wall Street is a thrilling exploration of greed, ambition, and the allure of the American Dream.
